Strategy

Most systems have unforeseen complexities that require flexibility in their approach as markets shift, behaviors change and the organization grows. Identifying these factors help guide the appropriate use of technology to achieve the best results.

Design

We help you reduce entropy by designing the solutions that make a difference in your organization. Simply functioning systems are not sufficient, they have hidden costs and can become a burden by themselves. Appropriate designs increase productivity by considering the overall systems experience, from user interaction, workflows, architecture, security, development, discovered constraints, support, devops and test automation and the future state roadmap.

Implementation

Implementations start small to allow a foundational architecture to develop before onboarding a larger team. We use the best fit management methodology to allow for tight feedback loops across all stakeholders. We automate our work to reduce the efforts for IT infrastructure, networking, deployments, QA/unit/functional testing and system resiliency
